About

This website is designed to help manage the Barbhie Boarding House more easily.

You can use it to:

  • Add and remove tenants
  • Record and update rent payments
  • View all tenant and payment records in one place

It helps keep everything organized, saves time, and makes it easier to track important information about the boarding house.
